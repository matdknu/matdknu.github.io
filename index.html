About Me

Matías Deneken is a Sociologist, Master in Sociology and Master Candidate in Data Science and Innovation. He is currently working at the Centre for Intercultural and Indigenous Studies (www.ciir.cl) and at the Observatory of Social Violence and Legitimacy (www.oles.cl). His interests are in Computational Social Science, Intergroup Relations and Natural Language Processing (NLP).

He is a user of R and Python.

Matías is a Fulbright Scholar, so he will start his PhD in the United States in 2027 at a university to be defined. His programme ideals are Social Psychology, Computational Social Science or Public Policy (with a focus on data science). Could you help him with a conversation about this?
