About Me

Matías Deneken is a Sociologist, Master in Sociology and Master Candidate in Data Science and Innovation.

He is currently working at the Centre for Intercultural and Indigenous Studies and at the Observatory of Social Violence and Legitimacy.

His interests are in Computational Social Science, Intergroup Relations, and Natural Language Processing (NLP).
